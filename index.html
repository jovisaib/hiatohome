About

Hiato creates moments of pause—where art, technology, and architecture converge to transform spaces into living, breathing experiences.

Based in Dubai and part of the Allometrik group, we specialize in designing and producing immersive installations that captivate audiences and redefine the relationship between people and their environments. From permanent architectural interventions to ephemeral event activations, we bring monumental digital experiences to unexpected contexts.
